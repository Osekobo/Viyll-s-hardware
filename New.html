About Kione Hardware

Kione Hardware has been a cornerstone of the Migori County community since 2010, providing high-quality construction materials, tools, and expert advice to both professionals and DIY enthusiasts.

Our mission is to empower builders, contractors, and homeowners with the tools and materials they need to bring their projects to life. We pride ourselves on our knowledgeable staff, competitive prices, and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Located in the heart of Migori, we serve customers from across the county and beyond, offering a wide range of products from basic hardware to specialized construction materials.
